Mind Makers

Computational Thinking school.

Mind Makers is a technology and creativity school for K-12 kids I helped to found in 2016. As a part of the Product Development team, we designed over 3 years worth of courses that include programing, algorithmic thinking and problem solving as subjects.

Today, Mind Makers' content accounts for more than 10 years worth of classes, and it is partnered with over 50 schools and has around 33 thousand students in all five regions of Brazil.
