Improving public services through design with the Gemeente Haarlem

Mijn Uitkeringen was a platform designed as part of my graduation project for the Hague University of Applied Sciences while working for the Haarlem Municipality. The project was done at the Innovation and Technology department, together with workers from the Work and Income department. The platform was created to help bridge the communication gap between residents and social workers. Apart from the digital platform, a concept for physical document exchange was proposed as a way to accelerate some processes.

  • Bridge the communication gap between municipality workers and Haarlem residents
  • Provide an allowance overview to the residents
  • Create a better way to exchange files and documents
